Our Mission

our mission is to estimate the total carbon emmision caused by an individual ,service,product expressed as carbon dioxide equivalent.The more is the emmision of carbon ,the more bigger the footprint becomesThe aim is to identify the major sources of emmision of carbon dioxide in a complete trip ,journey,or basically we could say in context to tourism ,so that actions can be taken to reduce or to compensate the emmision.

Our Vision

Our vision is to make people aware about their carbon footprint in a journey and take steps accordingly to reduce their carbon footprint .we can't completely diminish the carbon footprint but steps could be taken to nullify the effect of carbon emission by providing same equivalent of oxygen in return.
