Ways To Reduce The Carbon Footprint

1. Packing Light

Heavy luggage can impact the weight of the aircraft and requires more fuel, so be sure to pack light, taking only the essentials that you will need for your trip. If every passenger reduced the weight of their baggage this could help reduce the amount of fuel needed for flights, and also contribute to reducing the amount of waste in the country you are visiting. Instead of buying travel-size toiletries, for example, consider purchasing reusable clear bottles and pots which you can decant your larger bottles into, taking as much as you need for the duration of your trip.

2.Choose Fuel Efficient Vehicles

When shopping for a new car, look for fuel efficient vehicles with low greenhouse gas emissions. These cars can help the environment while potentially saving you money on fuel costs at the pump. Follow these tips:
1. Use EPA's Green Vehicle Guide to learn about vehicles that are more efficient and less polluting, including:

  • electric vehicles

  • plug-in hybrid electric vehicles

  • cleaner burning gasoline vehicles

  • hydrogen fuel cell vehicles

    • 2. Use the EPA's Fuel Economy and Environment Lable to compare diffrent vechicle models and find the most fuel efficient and environmentally friendly vechicle that meet your needs the information is also available on the point DOE and EPA website fuel economy.gov
